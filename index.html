I’m Kristiana, an Interactive Designer and Front-End Developer with a background in media and cultural studies. I’m currently trotting the globe while working for Mobiles Inc., a small company based in London, Ontario.

In addition to design and travel, I have a passion for visual art and an incessant love for varying shades of yellow.

I’m inspired by Miranda July, enlightened by Barbara Kruger, and forever captivated by all things high contrast.

Recent Projects

At the tail end of 2016, Mobials Inc announced it’s partnership with Kijiji. This partnership would allow for Mobials’ main product and review platform, Reviewsii, to be promoted and sold to Kijiji’s centralized network of digital communities. This campaign focused largely around the notion that the value consumers would receive through the Mobials/Kijiji partnership was so good it could only be described as magic.
