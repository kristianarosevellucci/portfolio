Portfolio / Reviewsii Meets Kijiji

A promotional campaign with a touch of magic.

At the tail end of 2016, Mobials Inc announced it’s partnership with Kijiji, the online classified advertising service. This partnership would allow for Mobials’ main product and review platform, better known as Reviewsii, to be promoted to Kijiji’s centralized network of digital communities while providing new customers with discounted access to Reviewsii’s services.

As a result, new design collateral (including a print booklet, pricing chart, web banners, and a website) were created to promote a partnership that is so good, it might as well be magic.

Mobials Inc.

The use of vehicles in the pricing chart was to signify Reviewsii’s ability to decrease the work load of consumers who enlisted in the help of the Reviewsii product. Why use a moped when you have access to a transport truck?

Making magic happen.

This campaign focused largely around the notion that the value consumers would receive through the Mobials/Kijiji partnership was so good it couldn’t possibly be described in any way other than to say it was magical. The above steps walk through the creation of the magicians hat that became the visual metaphor for this campaign. Initially a simple magic hat, I later added the Reviewsii, Kijiji, and Google icons in addition to the coloured superscript dots found throughout Kijiji’s logo to suggest that this campaign wasn’t created through ordinary magic.
