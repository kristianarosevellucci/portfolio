Portfolio / Identity Sketches

3 attempts to capture likeness and emotion.

Initially started as part of a design team building exercise during my time at Infotech Research Group, these identity sketches continued as a personal project to expand my illustration abilities. The initial project required each designer to depict the angry profile of another designer using only black and white in an image that could easily be transferred onto a mug. The initial portrait was completed using a series of grey and white lines, with the later illustrations consisting of 10 or less lines in the same colours.

Inspired by scribble artist Vince Low, these portraits are an attempt to capture basic human emotions and the profiles of the people on which these emotions appear using one the simplest, basic elements on art: the line.
