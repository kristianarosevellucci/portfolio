Personal Project

This project initially started as part of a design team building exercise during my time at Infotech Research Group and continued as a personal project. Each designer was required to depict the angry profile of a team member using only black and white. My original portrait (labelled as #2 on this page) was completed using a series of grey and white lines, with the later illustrations consisting of 10 or less lines in the same colours. 

Inspired by scribble artist Vince Low, these portraits are an attempt to capture basic human emotions and the profiles of the people on which these emotions appear using one the simplest elements in art: the line.
