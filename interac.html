Interac

Corporate website redesign

View Live Site

In 2019, Interac sought to make their website accessible, modern and engaging to both businesses and everyday consumers.

Interac is one of the most trusted financial brands in Canada, helping to facilitate secure financial transactions between merchants and consumers. In 2019, they engaged teams from Toronto agencies Zulu Alpha Kilo and TWG to redesign their website. We created a component based site that could be easily updated and maintained by the Interac team. We created a series of web components, made UX recommendations and collaborated on the art direction of the site.

Some Design Explorations

At the onset of the project, we completed a UI audit of the current Interac website to highlight any inconsistencies, determine key components and uncover weaknesses in the pre-existing visual system. We also reviewed suggested sitemaps from the Zulu Alpha Kilo team (display right).

Colour Palette & Typography

One of our biggest challenges on this site was working with a brand that had an inflexible and limited color palette and typography system. We opted to keep the majority of the site light and airy to help Interac seem more approachable and friendly than your average banking app.

One of the key deliverables of this project was a design system and component guide.

We presented our final design system as a component guide. The goal is to help remove any blindspots that the Interac developers, copy writers and internal team may have as they update the website in the future. It contains notes on topics such as development functionality, interactivity, word and character counts and design notes.
