Mobials Inc.
Rebrand

www.mobials.com

Sample text goes here

 In 2017 Mobials Inc. evolved significantly as a company. Starting the year with one application available in the automotive space, the company later saw this figure increase drastically with four products slated to be in the hands of consumers by year end. This project would introduce consumers to a completely new logo, brand identity and website. Departing from the original vibrant greens and middle greys, this rebrand sought to use colours, fonts and imagery that would represent Mobials’ as the professional, yet personality filled organization that it has become.

The Logo Evolution

As a company that provides software for an industry that relies heavily on the ever changing digital world, Mobials itself must be adaptable and constantly evolving. After working with employees to define company culture, I decided it was crucial to have a logo that represents this one very important trait. It was requested that the logo focus on the letter “M”, and in order to represent this letter alongside the fluid and constantly evolving nature of the company, I decided to take the logo in an abstract direction. The series of images below take you through some of the iterations that led to the final product.
