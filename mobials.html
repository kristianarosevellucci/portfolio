Getting a Facelift

Mobials Inc. is a software development company focused on bringing value to B2C companies and their customers - solving real world problems using the most cutting edge technologies. In 2016 Mobials Inc. evolved significantly as a company. They started the year with one application available for customer use and later saw this figure increase drastically with four products slated to be in the hands of customers by the end of 2017.

This rebrand would introduce consumers to a completely new logo, brand identity and website. Departing from the original vibrant greens and middle greys, this rebrand sought to use colours, fonts and imagery that would represent Mobials’ as the professional, yet personality filled organization that it is.

The Centre of Every Rebrand

As a company that provides software for an industry that relies heavily on the ever changing digital world, Mobials itself must be adaptable and constantly evolving. After working with employees to define company culture, I decided it was crucial to have a logo that represents this one very important trait. It was requested that the logo focus on the letter “M”, and in order to represent this letter alongside the fluid and constantly evolving nature of the company, I decided to take the logo is an abstract direction. The neighbouring images take you through some of the iterations we went through to arrive at the final product.
